Binangkal Recipe


Ingredients:


  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup White Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Salted Butter
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1/2 cup Evaporated Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sesame Seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla (optional)

    • Procedure:

    The dry and wet ingredients are mixed and the dough is divided into small balls.
    The dough balls are rolled in sesame seeds, then fried in oil on both sides over medium heat.
    Once done, the binangkal is drained on paper towels in order to absorb oil,
    and it's then served warm, often with coffee or hot chocolate as an afternoon snack.

    They say Binangkal is best when eaten hot!
